FAQ

Common questions

How are vendors verified on VendorBridge?+
Every supplier goes through a 7-point check: GST registration, Udyam/MSME certificate, bank account validation, address verification, trade references, product sample review, and founder identity verification.
Is VendorBridge free for buyers?+
Buyers can search and send quote requests for free. We earn a small facilitation fee on confirmed orders — so we're incentivised to find you the best match, not just any match.
How quickly will I receive quotes after posting an RFQ?+
Most RFQs receive their first verified quote within 4–8 business hours. Our 24-hour commitment covers getting you a minimum of 3 structured, comparable quotes from matched vendors.
Which industries and categories are covered?+
We currently cover plastics & polymers, metals & fabrication, packaging, textiles, electronics components, and industrial supplies — with new categories added quarterly based on demand.
Can I list my business as a verified supplier?+
Yes. Suppliers can apply through our vendor onboarding form. Listing is free during our early access period. Approval is subject to verification and typically takes 3–5 business days.
What happens if a supplier doesn't deliver as promised?+
All procurement through VendorBridge creates a digital paper trail — communication logs, order confirmations, and delivery records. Our resolution team reviews disputes and works toward a fair outcome.
